Project information

  • Category: Grad School
  • Class: Planning for Low Impact Development and Green Infrastructure
  • Semester: Spring 2021
  • URL: Project Paper, Presentation
  • Applied skills: LID and GI policy & planning strategies, nutrient loading calculations

This course presented a comprehensive approach to site planning and development that incorporates low-impact development (LID) approaches and green infrastructure (GI) techniques, including smart growth and sustainable development principles, affordable housing, transportation, and open space preservation. My final project explored a proposed affordable housing development in Cambridge, MA.

I examined some of the local stormwater regulations unique to Cambridge and the proposed LID and GI approaches included in the current site plans. I outlined recommendations for innovative strategies that could further reduce the impact of the proposed development, including expanding the proposed green roof and considering harvest and reuse opportunities.
