Project information

  • Category: Non-Profit Career
  • Organization: Living Classrooms Foundation
  • Role: Garden & Environment Education Coordinator
  • Location: Baltimore, MD
  • Dates: 2014-2016
  • Program URL: https://livingclassrooms.org/programs/baltimore-urban-gardening-students-school-program-bugs/

BUGS is an after-school program in East Baltimore that works with students from 2nd - 5th grade. As the garden and environmental education coordinator, my goal was to foster meaningful relationships with and amongst students and families and to develop an engaging program that gave students a unique opportunity to learn through exploration, play, and responsibility. In my role, I coordinated an annual student-run plant sale that raised funds for our summer programming.
