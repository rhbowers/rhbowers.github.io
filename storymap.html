Project information

  • Category: Grad School
  • Class: Advanced GIS, Web GIS
  • Semester: Fall 2020
  • URL: Storymap
  • App: Interactive Web Map
  • Applied skills: GIS, Web Mapping, Storymaps

I conducted a geospatial analysis that looked to answered the question: Where should future Green Stormwater Infrastructure projects in Pittsburgh be sited in order to emphasize environmental and socioeconomic equity?

I used Esri Storymaps to graphically tell the story of the project, including the development of an Interactive Web App that allows users to filter for neighborhoods, GI types, and priority levels, or to conduct their own spatial analysis. This project served as the basis for my Master's thesis.
