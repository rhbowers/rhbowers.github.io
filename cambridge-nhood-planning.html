Project information

  • Category: Internships
  • Organization: City of Cambridge Community Development Department (CDD)
  • Role: Graduate Community Planning Intern
  • Location: Cambridge, MA
  • Dates: 2020

In the summer of 2019, youth participants in the Mayor's Summer Youth Employment Program (MSYEP) collected qualitative and quantitative "public life" data in four of Cambridge's main squares. Three types of survey data were collected: Qualities of Public Space, Pedestrian Count, and Stationary Activity.

As a Community Planning intern, my role involved cleaning, preparing, analyzing, and presenting this data in order to draw insights about the relationships between design conditions and stationary activity, relationships between people moving through and people staying in areas, and factors that make spaces more sociable, enjoyable, or diverse. Results of this analysis have been used to pilot public realm projects in Cambridge in 2021.
