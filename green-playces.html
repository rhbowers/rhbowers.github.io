Project information

  • Category: Non-Profit Career
  • Organization: Grounded Strategies
  • Role: Project Manager
  • Location: Pittsburgh, PA
  • Dates: 2017-2019
  • Program Report: "Opportunities for Sustaining Green Playces"

When I started my role as Project Manager at Grounded, the Green Playces program - which aims to utilize vacant land to create a safer and more playful region with and for youth in the Pittsburgh region - had just finished its initial pilot programming. Through a Heinz Endowments grant, I led a team in a strategic planning process to reflect on the successes and challenges of the initial projects and to propose pathways for growing and sustaining the program. As a result of this process, we fundraised $500,000 for the program over the following two years.

In my role as Project Manager for Green Playces, I partnered with muncicipal agencies, town councils, local youth programs and community groups, and regional non-profits in a program that invested in youth-led vacant land reclamation. Under my leadership, the program was restructured to give youth meaningful opportunities to lead their communities in the collarborative design, building, and maintenance of vacant lot reclamation projects.
