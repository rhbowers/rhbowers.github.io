Project information

  • Category: Grad School
  • Class: Intro to GIS, Foundations of Policy and Planning
  • Semester: Fall 2019
  • URL: PosterPolicy Brief \
  • Applied skills: GIS, InDesign, Literature Review, Policy Development

To spatially identify environmental justice communities in Baltimore, I conducted a basic vulnerability analysis based on factors related to social and environmental vulnerabilities. I presented my project in a poster created in InDesign.

Additionally, I conducted a thorough literature and policy review to develop a policy brief that outlines a vision for equity-focused green infrastructure planning that addresses historic environmental injustice issues in the city.
